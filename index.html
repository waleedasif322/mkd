Pakistan holds the highest rates of preventable infant mortality in the world.

We onboard mothers in rural areas to get their children vaccinated.

Registration is easy through a voice automated or text-based system. Then we follow up with reminders to vaccinate and pick up rewards.

Brands partner with us to advertise directly to mothers.

Guaranteed customers through loyalty program.

Mothers receive products after children are vaccinated

Mothers continue to buy discounted products from brands for life.
